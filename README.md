# OSINT & Digital Investigations Portfolio

> Open-source intelligence investigations spanning geolocation, media verification, social networks, threat intelligence, corporate research, fraud, and humanitarian analysis — each case documented to professional reporting standards.

Every investigation in this portfolio is independently conducted from scratch. Evidence is collected, verified, corroborated, and synthesized into structured intelligence assessments using reproducible, professional-grade methodologies.

---

## Areas of Focus

| # | Domain | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | **Geolocation** | Pinpointing image and video origins using visual clues, satellite imagery, and geospatial analysis |
| 02 | **Image Verification** | Authenticating images — origin, context, and integrity — through OSINT and forensic techniques |
| 03 | **Video Investigations** | Verifying videos via frame analysis, geolocation, timeline reconstruction, and contextual corroboration |
| 04 | **Social Media Intelligence (SOCMINT)** | Mapping digital footprints, networks, and online communities from publicly available social data |
| 05 | **Threat Intelligence** | Profiling threat actors, tracking malicious infrastructure, and analyzing cyber threats via open sources |
| 06 | **Domain Investigations** | Examining DNS records, internet infrastructure, and ownership indicators to support investigations |
| 07 | **Corporate Intelligence** | Researching organizations, executives, ownership structures, and business activities through public records |
| 08 | **Fraud Investigations** | Identifying scams, deceptive operations, and fraudulent schemes using evidence-based methodologies |
| 09 | **Link Analysis** | Mapping relationships and associations between people, organizations, domains, and digital entities |
| 10 | **Public Records OSINT** | Collecting and verifying publicly available records to support intelligence gathering |
| 11 | **Humanitarian OSINT** | Applying investigative techniques to crisis response, conflict monitoring, and disaster analysis |
| 12 | **Healthcare OSINT** | Investigating public health information, scientific research, and medical intelligence through open sources |
| 13 | **Investigation Reporting** | Producing structured, evidence-based reports that communicate findings and intelligence assessments |

---

## Investigative Methodology

Every case follows a structured pipeline designed for rigor and reproducibility:

```
Research Question → Collection → Verification → Correlation
       → Analysis → Findings → Intelligence Assessment → Report
```

Each stage emphasizes source validation, evidence corroboration, and documented reasoning — the same standards applied in professional investigative and intelligence contexts.
