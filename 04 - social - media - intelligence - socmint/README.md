# 📡 Social Media Intelligence (SOCMINT)

> Gathering, analysing, and verifying publicly available information from social media platforms to support digital investigations and open-source intelligence operations — from a single profile to a fully mapped network.

Every investigation in this folder is conducted ethically using only publicly available information. Evidence is collected, verified, corroborated, and synthesized into structured intelligence assessments. No assumptions. No shortcuts. Evidence in, intelligence out.

---

## 🎯 What This Covers

| Technique | What It Involves |
|---|---|
| **Profile Analysis** | Examining public profiles to extract identity indicators, behavioural patterns, and digital history |
| **Network Mapping** | Visualising relationships, connections, and associations between accounts and communities |
| **Community Analysis** | Investigating online groups, forums, and communities to understand structure and influence |
| **Content Verification** | Confirming the authenticity, origin, and context of social media posts and shared media |
| **Behavioural Analysis** | Identifying patterns in posting activity, engagement, and online behaviour over time |
| **Trend Analysis** | Monitoring and analysing emerging narratives, hashtags, and information flows |
| **Influence Assessment** | Evaluating the reach, credibility, and impact of accounts and content within a network |
| **Information Verification** | Cross-referencing claims and content against independent open-source evidence |
| **Digital Footprint Analysis** | Tracing an individual's or organisation's presence and activity across multiple platforms |
| **Evidence Documentation** | Recording all findings in structured, verifiable, and reproducible investigation reports |

## ⚖️ Ethical Framework

All investigations in this folder are conducted in strict accordance with OSINT ethical principles:

- **Public data only** — No private, restricted, or non-public information is accessed or used
- **Proportionality** — Collection is limited to what is necessary for the investigation
- **No harassment** — Investigations do not target, expose, or harm private individuals
- **Legal compliance** — All methods comply with applicable laws and platform terms of service
- **Responsible disclosure** — Sensitive findings are handled with appropriate care and discretion
