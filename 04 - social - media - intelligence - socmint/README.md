# Social Media Intelligence (SOCMINT)

Gathering, analyzing, and verifying publicly available information from social media platforms to support digital investigations and intelligence gathering.

This repository documents my journey in Social Media Intelligence (SOCMINT) through practical case studies, investigative methodologies, and real-world analysis.

## Areas of Focus

* Profile Analysis
* Network Mapping
* Community Analysis
* Content Verification
* Behavioral Analysis
* Trend Analysis
* Influence Assessment
* Information Verification
* Digital Footprint Analysis
* Evidence Documentation & Reporting

## Investigation Methodology

1. Identify relevant social media platforms and sources.
2. Collect publicly available information.
3. Analyze profiles, posts, interactions, and networks.
4. Verify findings across multiple sources.
5. Correlate evidence and establish relationships.
6. Document conclusions with supporting evidence.
