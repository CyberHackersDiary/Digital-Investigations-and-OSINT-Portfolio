# 🔍 Fraud Investigations

> Investigating scams, deceptive activities, and fraudulent online operations through open-source intelligence and evidence-based analysis — from a single suspicious indicator to a fully documented fraud intelligence assessment.

Every investigation in this folder starts with a suspected fraud activity or deceptive operation and ends with a documented, reproducible intelligence finding. No assumptions. No shortcuts. Evidence in, intelligence out.

---

## 🎯 What This Covers

| Technique | What It Involves |
|---|---|
| **Scam Analysis** | Identifying and dissecting the mechanics, patterns, and indicators of online scam operations |
| **Fraud Detection** | Recognising deceptive activities through evidence-based open-source investigation techniques |
| **Website Verification** | Assessing website legitimacy, registration history, and technical indicators of fraudulent intent |
| **Identity Deception Analysis** | Investigating fake personas, impersonation tactics, and fabricated identity indicators |
| **Financial Fraud Research** | Examining publicly available evidence of fraudulent financial schemes and deceptive practices |
| **Social Engineering Analysis** | Identifying manipulation tactics, pretexting strategies, and psychological deception techniques |
| **Risk Assessment** | Evaluating the threat level, victim impact, and operational scope of fraudulent activities |
| **Intelligence Correlation** | Cross-referencing fraud indicators across multiple platforms and independent sources |
| **Evidence Collection** | Systematically gathering and preserving open-source evidence to support fraud investigations |
| **Evidence Documentation** | Recording all findings in structured, verifiable, and reproducible investigation reports |

## 🔬 Investigative Methodology

Each investigation follows a disciplined, repeatable eight-stage workflow:

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               INVESTIGATIVE METHODOLOGY                      ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                              ║
║  01  RESEARCH QUESTION                                       ║
║      Define the suspected fraud activity and scope of        ║
║      investigation                                           ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  02  COLLECTION                                              ║
║      Gather publicly available fraud indicators, website     ║
║      data, profiles, and supporting evidence                 ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  03  VERIFICATION                                            ║
║      Validate source credibility and evidence integrity      ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  04  CORRELATION                                             ║
║      Cross-reference fraud indicators across multiple        ║
║      platforms and independent intelligence sources          ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  05  ANALYSIS                                                ║
║      Apply fraud investigation techniques to identify        ║
║      deception patterns, false claims, and risk indicators   ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  06  FINDINGS                                                ║
║      Establish key conclusions on fraud activity,            ║
║      deceptive intent, and associated risk                   ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  07  INTELLIGENCE ASSESSMENT                                 ║
║      Assess confidence level, fraud typology, victim         ║
║      impact, and alternative explanations                    ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  08  REPORT                                                  ║
║      Document conclusions in a structured, reproducible      ║
║      fraud intelligence report                               ║
║                                                              ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Every conclusion is evidence-backed. Every step is reproducible.

## ⚠️ Responsible Use

All investigations in this folder are conducted strictly using open-source intelligence and publicly available data:

- **Legal compliance** — All methods comply with applicable laws and platform terms of service
- **Public data only** — No unauthorised access to private, restricted, or confidential information
- **No vigilantism** — Findings are documented for intelligence purposes only and not used for direct action
- **Responsible handling** — Sensitive findings are handled with appropriate care and discretion
- **Educational purpose** — All case studies are conducted for learning and portfolio demonstration only
