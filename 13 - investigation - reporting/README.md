## 🔬 Investigative Methodology

Each report follows a disciplined, repeatable eight-stage workflow:

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               INVESTIGATIVE METHODOLOGY                      ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                              ║
║  01  RESEARCH QUESTION                                       ║
║      Define the investigation scope, objectives, and         ║
║      intended audience for the report                        ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  02  COLLECTION                                              ║
║      Gather, organise, and catalogue all relevant            ║
║      evidence and supporting documentation                   ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  03  VERIFICATION                                            ║
║      Validate source credibility, evidence integrity,        ║
║      and factual accuracy of all collected material          ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  04  CORRELATION                                             ║
║      Cross-reference evidence across multiple sources        ║
║      to identify patterns, gaps, and key connections         ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  05  ANALYSIS                                                ║
║      Interpret the evidence to develop well-reasoned         ║
║      findings and intelligence conclusions                   ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  06  FINDINGS                                                ║
║      Structure conclusions into a clear, logical narrative   ║
║      supported by verified evidence                          ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  07  INTELLIGENCE ASSESSMENT                                 ║
║      Assess confidence levels, analytical limitations,       ║
║      and alternative interpretations of the evidence         ║
║                              ↓                               ║
║  08  REPORT                                                  ║
║      Produce a professional, structured, and reproducible    ║
║      investigation report ready for the intended audience    ║
║                                                              ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Every conclusion is evidence-backed. Every step is reproducible.

## 📐 Report Structure

Every investigation report in this folder follows a consistent professional framework:

| Section | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Executive Summary** | High-level overview of the investigation, key findings, and conclusions |
| **Investigation Scope** | Defines the objectives, boundaries, and research questions of the investigation |
| **Methodology** | Documents the investigative process, tools used, and analytical approach |
| **Evidence & Findings** | Presents collected evidence, analysis, and key investigative conclusions |
| **Intelligence Assessment** | Evaluates confidence levels, limitations, and alternative interpretations |
| **Recommendations** | Outlines actionable next steps or areas for further investigation |
| **Appendices** | Includes supporting evidence, screenshots, timelines, and reference material |
